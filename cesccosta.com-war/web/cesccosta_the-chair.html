The Chair

Barcelona, march 2016.

Previous Next

The moment, the chair

In a painting exhibition (19:11 GMT+1), march 25, in Barcelona.

I sat, watching a picture, I noticed the empty chair in a corner, and in the background a painting and two, with bluish background. Amid a space missing an item, it was empty. I framed the people in the background and the chair, waiting for someone to appear in the central space, do it soon, I shot three times in three positions.

Light, focus, lens and camera

I had enough time to measure light, I always selected spot metering in the camera, so I measured white wall, and I over exposed +2. I calibrated my camera so perfectly when I'll have detail in the lights. I focused, AF, on the chair, I was interested in this element, I mounted the 85mm 1.4 lens, the diaphragm was 3.3, a moderate distance from the first subject (chair) of 4 meters aprox. The rest seems slightly out of focus, and in the middle distance even detailed.

I shoot automatic, aperture priority "A" and automatic ISO (4500) almost always, I control the light through the exposure compensation.

In this case, I shot 3 times.

The camera was a Nikon D810, You can check the settings and how to select and use, you can also download my settings if you wish.

The lens is a Nikkor 85mm 1.4 AF-S
